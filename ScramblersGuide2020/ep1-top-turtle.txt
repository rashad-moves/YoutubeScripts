Video Script

i got top turtle with top crab ride
  1. he rolls over, get to SC or top n/s or chairsit
  2. if he starts trying to stand up
    -rnc and back hooks 
    -force him to front roll
    -force a sitout by pulling him into my lap or circular rotation
  3. he's statuing. double clubs, wrist ride, belly him out, elbow pressure back of his head for rnc.  if he tries to turtle, back hooks

transition to body lock with top turtle
  1. step in for back hooks as I lift him up. works best if he keeps his feet/knees on the ground so there's space.
  2. he stays balled up, gut wrench to a side for SC/charsit or N/S

transition to head in armpit with thigh pry
  1. chop tilt to rear triangle
  2. near side wrist ride for hammerlock
    -if hands stuck
      -forearm over bicep so he chooses between unnofficial bicep slicer pressure or hammerlock
      -butt drag plus step over his head to force him to a side kimura
  3. far side pinky for SC or n/s 
  4. half nelson to n/s choke
  5. he's driving back into me, chair sit back take with rnc / pull him into my lap
  6. he front rolls, get top n/s

transition to ankle ride using thigh pry as butt drag
  1. far side wrist ride / hammerlock
   -if he rolls over to protect that arm, tech mount > s mount
     -cant make it to tech mount? near side hook comes in for 3/4 mount
  2. power half to get crossbody hook in
  -break him down / pull him in for back take
   -cant break him down?  calf slicer.
     -if he rolls over or into me, still calfslicer but can go for crossbody series
     -bananasplit, hammerlock, twister, rnc + back
  3. if hes driving into me, get a seat belt grip or thigh pry plus far shoulder/dragon sleeper works and pull him into my lap for back take 
  4. back to powerhalf, use my ankle ride to tilt his head to the mat, chop tilt works as well. try and flatten him out. preferbly get near side hook in, ankle ride shin crabs his far leg and comes over my near leg
    -if you miss the near side hook, get a top crab with him flattened out
    -can work with double wrist ride

front head lock series.
  1. hes hand fighting. smash his head to the mat so that he gets rocked or posts his hands to the mat.  use that moment for a go behind and do all the previous stuff.
    -to secure the go behind, transition to head in armpit
  2. rnc gullotine, gator roll to mount. mounted gullotine if grip gets loose
    -gun choke as follow up
  3. diaz choke
    -marcelotine as follow up
  4. anaconda choke
    -caveman necktie
  5. darce choke
    -nelson neck tie
    -peruvian neck tie
      -armbar if hes not choking
      -rear triangle if his head pops out
